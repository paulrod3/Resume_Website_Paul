About Me

My introduction

My interest in technology started at a young age, but I initially pursued other fields such as Project management and business. However, I found that these careers did not provide the same level of fulfillment and excitement as working with technology did.

Eventually, after a close friend helped me code my first mini-game using Python, I decided to take the plunge and dive into the world of software development.

Although I am still in the early stages of my software development journey, through self-study and Instructor-led courses, I am gaining proficiency in various programming languages, such as Java, JavaScript, and Python. I have been practicing by building small projects and hope to one day develop programs that can improve people's lives. I enjoy exploring new concepts and experimenting with different approaches to problem-solving.

In my free time, you can find me spending quality moments with my family, playing video games, or shredding a trail on my mountain bike. My experience in the Marine Corps has instilled in me the importance of discipline, teamwork, and adaptability, which have proven to be valuable traits in my journey as a software developer.

I am excited about the future of technology and the impact that software development can have on the world. I am committed to using my skills and knowledge to contribute to innovative and impactful software solutions.
